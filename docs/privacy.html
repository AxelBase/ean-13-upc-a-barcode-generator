Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 26, 2025

1. We Collect Nothing

This tool runs 100% in your browser. When you generate a barcode, nothing is sent to any server — not even your IP address. Your input numbers, generated SVGs, PNGs, and clipboard actions stay entirely on your device.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We explicitly do not collect, store, or process:

  • Barcode numbers you enter
  • Generated images or files
  • IP addresses, browser type, or location
  • Usage analytics or tracking pixels
  • Cookies, localStorage persistence, or session data

3. How the Tool Works Offline

All barcode generation uses bwip-js running locally. Even if you're offline, the tool works perfectly. No internet connection = no possibility of data transmission.

4. No Third-Party Services

We do not use:

  • Google Analytics
  • Facebook Pixel
  • Advertising networks
  • External fonts or tracking scripts

Only essential, self-hosted assets are loaded.

5. Your Data, Your Control

You own everything you create. Downloads go straight to your device. Clipboard copies stay in your browser. We have zero access.

6. Security

Since no data leaves your device, there is no server to hack, no database to breach, and no logs to leak. Your usage is completely private by design.

7. Children's Privacy

This tool is safe for all ages. No personal information is ever requested or collected.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted here with the new effective date.

9. Contact

This tool is independently developed and privacy-first by design. Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.

Your privacy is not a feature — it’s the foundation. We built this tool the way the web should be: powerful, free, and completely private.
